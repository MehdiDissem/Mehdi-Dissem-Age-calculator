Age calculator, funny facts and zodiac sign preview.

In this project, I went through the steps gradually, as we have first a preview on the age
in hours, seconds and days, with an alert that shows when the input is wrong.
After that, there will be a funny button that shows a picture or meme, with the top listened song 
in that decade, feel free to see what each decade has to offer (starting from 1960).
Another button will emerge that takes the days and months inputs and shows your zodiac sign with 
a description about it, which leads to another button with the best match of your zodiac sign.
